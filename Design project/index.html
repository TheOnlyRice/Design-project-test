What is super sandwich?


Super Sandwich is a family run business founded in 1988 by Jerry and Jocelyne Lam. It was later taken over by their children, Jenny and Mathis in 2004. Super Sandwich is for a quick bite during lunch hour and can be available throughout the day.

Super Sandwich is a small depanneur located in downtown Montreal in Le Cartir building. Their main business is selling sandwiches. Their customers are mainly students from McgGill university, office workers and construction workers.
